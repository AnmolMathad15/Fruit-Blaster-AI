# 🏯 DOJO GATE

### *Classic Mode*

**Difficulty:** ⭐ Easy

**Description:**
The training grounds where every Guardian begins their journey. Master the fundamentals of fruit slicing and sharpen your reflexes.

**Gameplay:**

* ⏳ Unlimited Time
* ❤️ 3 Lives
* 🍉 Slice fruits to earn points
* 💣 Avoid bombs—they cost one life
* ⚡ Build combos for higher scores

**Objective:**
Learn the basics, improve your accuracy, and become familiar with the Sacred Blade.

---

# 🎋 BAMBOO GROVE

### *Zen Mode*

**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ Medium

**Description:**
A peaceful bamboo forest where calmness and precision are the keys to mastery. Every movement should be smooth and deliberate.

**Gameplay:**

* ⏳ **2 Minute Timer**
* 🍉 Fruits appear at a calm, rhythmic pace
* 💣 Very few bombs appear
* 🎯 Accuracy is more important than speed
* ⚡ Maintain long combo chains for bonus points

**Objective:**
Achieve the highest score through precision, rhythm, and flawless control within the time limit.

---

# 🔥 CRIMSON TEMPLE

### *Arcade Mode*

**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ Hard

**Description:**
An intense trial inside the Crimson Temple where speed, reflexes, and quick decision-making determine your fate.

**Gameplay:**

* ⏳ **90 Second Timer**
* 🍉 Fruits spawn rapidly
* 💣 Bombs appear frequently
* ⚡ High combo multipliers
* 🎁 Special bonus fruits award extra points

**Objective:**
Score as many points as possible before time runs out.

---

# 🌕 MOON SHRINE

### *Survival Mode*

**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Expert

**Description:**
Beneath the full moon, endless waves of enchanted fruits challenge your endurance. Only the strongest Guardians survive.

**Gameplay:**

* ♾️ Endless Gameplay
* 📈 Difficulty increases over time
* ⚡ Faster fruit speed
* 💣 Increasing bomb frequency
* 🏆 Highest survival time is recorded

**Objective:**
Survive for as long as possible while maintaining your score and accuracy.

---

# 👑 IMPERIAL PALACE

### *Challenge Mode*

**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Master

**Description:**
The Emperor's final trial. Complete unique missions designed to test every skill you have mastered.

**Gameplay:**

* 🎯 Mission-Based Challenges
* ⚔️ Unique objectives each round
* ⏳ Time-limited tasks
* 🏅 Exclusive rewards and achievements
* 👑 Designed for elite Guardians

**Example Challenges:**

* Slice 100 fruits without missing
* Reach a 75-hit combo
* Score 5,000 points in 60 seconds
* Slice only the required fruit type
* Complete a bomb-heavy survival trial

**Objective:**
Conquer every challenge and earn the title of **Guardian of the Sacred Jade Orchard**.
