# 🎮 GAMEPLAY RULES

The Crimson Temple uses the same core mechanics as Fruit Blaster, but with a higher difficulty and faster pace.

## Objective

Slice as many legendary Infernal Fruits as possible.

Avoid slicing the Infernal Dragon Core (bomb).

Earn the highest score while maintaining combos.

---

## Fruit Behaviour

• Fruits launch upward from the bottom of the screen.
• Fruits follow smooth natural arc trajectories.
• Fruits rotate while flying.
• Fruits may overlap naturally.
• Fruits spawn from random horizontal positions.
• Every fruit is fully sliceable.

---

## Fruit Spawn Rate

Compared to Moon Shrine:

• Increase fruit spawn rate by approximately 40%.
• Reduce empty time between waves.
• Launch frequent multi-fruit waves.
• Randomize every wave.

Possible wave patterns:

• Single Fruit
• Double Fruit
• Triple Fruit
• Quadruple Fruit
• Five Fruits
• Mixed Fruit Cluster
• Large Combo Wave

Gameplay should always feel active.

---

## Bomb Behaviour

The Infernal Dragon Core replaces normal bombs.

Bombs appear more frequently than in Moon Shrine.

Possible patterns:

• Single Bomb
• Bomb + 2 Fruits
• Bomb + 3 Fruits
• Double Bomb
• Late-game Triple Bomb

Bombs should never create impossible situations.

Players must always have a reaction window.

---

## Sword Controls

Mouse movement controls the Infernal Dragon Blade.

Every fast swipe creates:

• Dragon flame trail
• Ember particles
• Heat distortion
• Dragon spirit slash effect

The sword should feel smooth with zero noticeable input delay.

---

## Slice Detection

A fruit is sliced when the sword trail intersects its collider.

The slice should:

Split fruit into two realistic halves.

Apply natural physics.

Spawn infernal juice.

Spawn ember particles.

Play slice animation.

Award score instantly.

---

## Combo System

Slice multiple fruits with one continuous swipe.

3 Fruits = Small Combo

5 Fruits = Great Combo

7 Fruits = Dragon Combo

10+ Fruits = Infernal Combo

Higher combos award:

Bonus score

Extra particles

Dragon visual effects

Special sound effects

---

## Score System

Single Fruit = 1 Point

Combo Bonus = Additional Points

Perfect Combo = Extra Multiplier

Long Combo Chains increase score multiplier.

---

## Miss Rules

If a fruit falls off-screen unsliced:

Lose 1 Life.

Player begins with 3 Lives.

Game ends when all lives are lost.

Bombs falling safely do NOT reduce lives.

---

## Bomb Rules

If the player slices the Infernal Dragon Core:

Immediate Game Over.

Play volcanic explosion.

Brief screen shake.

Display final score.

Offer Restart and Main Menu.

---

## Difficulty Progression

Every 30–45 seconds:

Increase fruit speed.

Increase fruit count.

Increase bomb frequency.

Increase combo opportunities.

Gameplay should become more challenging while remaining fair.

---

## Performance

Maintain stable 60 FPS.

No lag.

No missed collision detection.

Smooth animations.

Instant input response.

Optimized particle effects.

Gameplay clarity always takes priority over visual effects.
