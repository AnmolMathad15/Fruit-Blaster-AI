# PAGE 2 — WARRIOR'S AWAKENING
## Fruit Blaster AI

# Objective

Design the second screen of Fruit Blaster AI.

This is NOT a settings page.

This is NOT a camera permission page.

This is NOT a webcam calibration page.

It is an interactive cinematic onboarding experience that introduces the player to the game's core mechanic:

"The player's real hand becomes the legendary sword."

The page should feel like an initiation ceremony inside an ancient mystical kingdom.

The player should forget they are granting camera access.

Instead, they should feel like they are awakening an ancient weapon that has been waiting centuries for its next guardian.

The entire experience should feel worthy of a AAA game.

---

# Story

After pressing "Play Now" on the landing page...

The screen fades.

The camera flies through drifting clouds.

Floating lanterns pass by.

The music becomes quieter.

The clouds slowly open.

A breathtaking floating mountain kingdom appears.

The player arrives at the Hall of Awakening.

A legendary jade sword rests on an ancient stone pedestal.

The sword has remained dormant for centuries.

Only a worthy guardian can awaken it.

The player is that guardian.

To awaken the sword...

The player must raise their real hand.

This is actually the computer vision calibration process hidden inside the game's story.

---

# Core Gameplay Introduction

Fruit Blaster AI is controlled entirely by hand gestures.

There are no mouse controls during gameplay.

There are no touch controls.

The player's real hand controls the sword.

This page must teach that concept naturally.

Without tutorials.

Without large instruction popups.

The moment the hand is detected...

The legendary sword begins following the player's movements.

The player instantly understands:

"My hand IS the sword."

That realization should be magical.

# Camera Area

This is the hero section.

Instead of a rectangular webcam...

Create an enormous Ancient Jade Mirror.

The mirror acts as the live camera.

The frame should be carved from jade and decorated with dragons, clouds, gold ornaments and ancient engravings.

It should glow softly.

Inside the mirror the live webcam appears.

When no hand is detected:

The mirror remains calm.

Small jade particles float.

When the hand appears:

The mirror glows brighter.

Golden energy spreads around the frame.

---

# Hand Tracking

Never display raw MediaPipe landmarks.

Instead:

Represent each hand joint as glowing jade nodes.

Connect them using thin golden energy lines.

Everything should look magical.

When the player moves:

Create:

jade energy

petals

tiny sparks

floating leaves

The trail disappears smoothly.
