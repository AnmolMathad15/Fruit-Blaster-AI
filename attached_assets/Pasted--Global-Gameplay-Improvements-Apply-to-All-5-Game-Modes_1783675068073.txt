# Global Gameplay Improvements (Apply to All 5 Game Modes)

These improvements must be applied consistently across **Dojo Gate, Bamboo Grove, Moon Shrine, Crimson Temple, and Imperial Heaven Palace** to provide a polished, premium gameplay experience.

## 1. Improve Hand Tracking

* Optimize hand detection for maximum accuracy and responsiveness.
* Sword movement should follow the player's fingertip with **minimal latency**.
* Reduce jitter, shaking, and sudden jumps.
* Smooth the tracking while maintaining fast response.
* If the hand is briefly lost, recover tracking immediately without noticeable delays.
* Prioritize stable gameplay at high frame rates (target **60 FPS** where possible).

## 2. Sword Ready Before Game Starts

* Detect the player's hand before gameplay begins.
* Display the selected sword attached to the player's index finger immediately after hand detection.
* The sword should remain visible and follow the finger while waiting for the game to begin.
* Do not spawn fruits until the countdown is complete.

## 3. Countdown Sequence

Before every game starts:

* Display a large animated countdown:

  * **3**
  * **2**
  * **1**
  * **GO!**
* Each number should appear for approximately **1 second**.
* Add subtle scaling/fade animations for each number.
* Play a countdown sound for 3, 2, and 1.
* Play an energetic "GO!" sound effect when the game starts.
* Begin fruit spawning only after the **GO!** animation finishes.

## 4. Smooth Gameplay

Ensure gameplay feels fluid in every mode.

* Maintain stable frame rates.
* Eliminate lag, stuttering, and frame drops.
* Keep animations smooth and responsive.
* Optimize particle effects and shadows to avoid performance issues.
* Prevent input delay between hand movement and sword movement.

## 5. Game Flow

The complete gameplay sequence should be:

1. Player selects a game mode.
2. Camera opens.
3. Hand detection initializes.
4. Sword appears on the player's fingertip.
5. Wait until a hand is detected.
6. Show the countdown:

   * 3
   * 2
   * 1
   * GO!
7. Start spawning fruits.
8. Begin scoring and gameplay.

## 6. Premium Experience

The game should feel polished from start to finish:

* Instant and reliable hand tracking.
* Responsive sword movement.
* Smooth animations and transitions.
* Fast loading between menus and game modes.
* No visual glitches, flickering, or input lag.
* Consistent gameplay quality across all five themed worlds.

**Goal:** Deliver a responsive, AAA-quality Fruit Ninja–style experience where the sword feels like a natural extension of the player's hand from the moment the game begins.
