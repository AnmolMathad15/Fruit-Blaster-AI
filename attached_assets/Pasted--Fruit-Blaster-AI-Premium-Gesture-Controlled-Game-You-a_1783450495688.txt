Gameplay

The user controls a glowing sword using ONLY their index finger.

The webcam appears behind the game.

The sword follows the user's fingertip in real time.

Fruits fly from the bottom with realistic physics.

If the sword intersects a fruit:

fruit splits into two halves
juice particles explode
score increases
combo increases
sound plays
screen slightly shakes

Bombs must reduce lives or end the game.

Game runs at 60 FPS.
