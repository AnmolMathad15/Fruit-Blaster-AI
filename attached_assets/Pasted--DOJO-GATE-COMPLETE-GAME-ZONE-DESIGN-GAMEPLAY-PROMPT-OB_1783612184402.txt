# 🏯 DOJO GATE — COMPLETE GAME ZONE DESIGN & GAMEPLAY PROMPT

## OBJECTIVE

Create an ultra-premium **Dojo Gate** game zone inspired by ancient Japanese martial arts temples. This level should feel like the sacred training ground of legendary samurai masters where discipline, precision, honor, and balance are more important than brute strength.

The experience must be calm, elegant, and immersive while remaining extremely exciting and satisfying to play. Every slice should feel clean, precise, and rewarding.

This is a premium Fruit Ninja–style experience with AAA-quality visuals, smooth gameplay, and polished animations.

# DOJO GATE RULES

## Objective

Slice as many sacred fruits as possible while demonstrating the precision and discipline of a true samurai.

---

### Scoring

• Slice 1 fruit = +1 point
• Combo of 3 fruits = Bonus points
• Combo of 5+ fruits = Higher bonus
• Perfect combo without misses = Precision Bonus
• Consecutive successful slices increase combo multiplier

---

### Avoid the Cursed Oni Mask

Never slice the Cursed Oni Mask.

If the Oni Mask is hit:

• The demon seal breaks.
• The round ends immediately.
• The player loses the current run.

---

### Miss Limit

Players may miss **up to 3 fruits**.

Missing a **4th fruit** ends the game.

---

### Combo Rewards

Reward players for precision:

• x2 Combo
• x3 Combo
• x5 Combo
• Master Combo
• Grandmaster Combo
• Zen Master Combo

Higher combos grant more points and satisfying visual feedback.

---

### Gameplay Philosophy

Victory is achieved through:

• Precision
• Patience
• Timing
• Discipline
• Focus

Wild or careless swiping should be less effective than controlled, deliberate cuts.

The Dojo Gate should feel like the sacred training grounds of history's greatest samurai masters. Every fruit should appear abundant, vibrant, and satisfying to slice. The result should be a premium, polished, AAA-quality Fruit Ninja experience where every swipe embodies discipline, precision, and the timeless spirit of the dojo.
